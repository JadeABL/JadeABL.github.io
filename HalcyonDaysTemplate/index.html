History

Jade ABL is a Canadian company specialized in Field Examinations for Asset Based Lending financing. The company was co-founded by Carmie Cammalleri and Lucy Conte in 2013 serving the Greater Montreal Area.
It has been accredited by a number of Canadian and US Lenders to conduct field examinations since 2013.
In 2015, Allen Bacarro joined the company serving the Greater Toronto Area and the rest of Canada.

Value-Added Service

Examination

As part of its standard field examination procedures in examining the Borrowing Base,
JADE ABL aims to leverage its expertise, experience and industry knowledge by providing insights on the Borrower’s operations and the Borrower’s relationships with their customers and suppliers.

Analysis

Over the years, the Partners developed analytical tools and techniques to effectively and efficiently conduct the examination, document the procedures and report the results.

Presentation

Presenting information in an organized, brief and concise fashion is key to making a report readable. JADE ABL has the experience to sift through loads of information, separate the trivial and highlight areas of concern to the various readers of the report.
